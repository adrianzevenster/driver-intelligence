# Melbourne ERS Deployment
Melbourne has moderate ERS value with one clear DRS zone and several medium-grade acceleration zones.

Key deployment zones:
- Main straight (Turn 16 to Turn 1): Full deployment from lakeside hairpin exit. Primary zone. Good harvest from Turn 16 braking to complement.
- Turn 3 to Turn 4: Light deployment; helps front stability through braking chicane, adds minimal lap time.

Key harvest zones:
- Turn 1 heavy braking: Primary harvest. Good regen yield from high-speed braking zone.
- Turn 9 chicane and Turn 15 hairpin: Secondary harvest points; both produce reasonable regen from medium-speed braking.

Battery management thresholds:
- SOC below 0.25 at Turn 15: reduce main-straight deployment to 70% on the following lap. Cool temperatures (20–25°C race) improve regen efficiency, so recovery is faster than at higher-temperature circuits.
- SOC below 0.12: full harvest. Alert engineer.
- Safety car deployment mode: under safety car, switch to maximum harvest. The field bunches up; every percentage point of SOC recovered during safety car translates directly to straight-line advantage at restart.
