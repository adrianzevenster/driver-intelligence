# Silverstone Track Intelligence
Silverstone has high-speed direction changes and heavy front-left load through Maggotts, Becketts and Chapel. Front-left degradation can accelerate when track temperature is high and the driver carries entry understeer into long loaded corners.

Key sectors:
- Sector 1: Abbey, Farm, Village and Loop. Braking stability matters into Village.
- Sector 2: Brooklands, Luffield, Copse, Maggotts and Becketts. Front-left thermal load is the dominant tire risk.
- Sector 3: Chapel, Hangar Straight, Stowe, Vale and Club. ERS deployment on Chapel exit and Hangar Straight is high value.

Intervention notes: if front-left wear exceeds expected curve and grip falls below 0.62, prepare pit window. If crosswind rises near Copse or Stowe, avoid over-committing turn-in.
