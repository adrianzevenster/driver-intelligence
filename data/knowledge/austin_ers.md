# Austin (COTA) ERS Deployment
COTA has two strong deployment zones and good harvest from Turn 1 braking. The circuit is moderate ERS value overall.

Key deployment zones:
- Main straight (Turn 20 to Turn 1): Full deployment from Turn 20 exit. Primary zone; DRS available. Long straight adds 0.3–0.4 s.
- Back straight (Turns 11–12): Secondary deployment from Turn 11 hairpin exit to Turn 12 braking. DRS zone. Adds 0.2 s.

Key harvest zones:
- Turn 1 braking from high speed (high point): Primary regen. The elevation and high entry speed produce good brake energy.
- Turn 11 hairpin: Secondary regen.

Battery management thresholds:
- SOC below 0.25 at Turn 19 (final hairpin): reduce back-straight deployment on following lap. Maintain main-straight deployment.
- SOC below 0.13: full harvest. Alert engineer.
- Note on Texas weather: if ambient temperature rises above 35°C (common in afternoon races), battery thermal management becomes a secondary concern. Monitor battery temp; if warnings trigger, reduce deployment as per Singapore protocol.
